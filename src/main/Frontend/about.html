About BC Assist

BC-Assist was created by a Bloomfield College IT student worker who identified and dealt with common challenges students faced when navigating the school's resources. The goal of BC-Assist is to provide students with a single, easy-to-navigate platform where they can find all the information they need without having to navigate through the sometimes frustrating MSU website.

This platform aims to streamline access to important resources like the academic calendar, frequently asked questions, and more, with a focus on making everything easily accessible and helpful.
